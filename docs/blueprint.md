# **App Name**: Ashim Scholars

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Content Display: Render key information sections including the hero banner, institutional statistics (students, alumni, hafiz), and core advantages with clear calls to action.
- News & Articles Showcase: Display the latest news and articles in a structured feed, with options to view full details for each entry.
- Educational Program Catalog: Present detailed information about various educational programs offered by the institution.
- Online Inquiry & Application Form: Provide a user-friendly form for prospective students or parents to submit inquiries or start an application process, with links to download essential documents.
- Interactive Contact & Location: Display comprehensive contact details, including address, phone number, and email, potentially integrated with a map for easy location finding.
- Video Profile Integration: Feature an embedded video to offer a visual and auditory introduction to the institution's facilities and mission.
- AI Inquiry Guidance Tool: Assist users in crafting effective inquiries about programs and admissions by providing a tool that suggests relevant questions and essential information to include based on common user needs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep forest green (#4A862D). This rich green conveys growth, heritage, and tranquility, fitting for an educational Islamic institution. It provides a stable and authoritative base for the design.
- Background color: Dark desaturated green (#171F14). A very dark background with a subtle green hue ensures high contrast for the primary color while maintaining a serene and distinguished ambiance.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#E8C830). A vibrant yet earthy golden yellow is used for interactive elements and highlights. It evokes a sense of excellence, warmth, and acts as a clear call to action against the dark green backdrop.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif). A modern, objective, and highly legible sans-serif font ensures clarity and readability for all informational and academic content.
- Utilize clean, minimalist line icons, often enclosed within soft-cornered shapes. Icons should subtly echo the primary and accent colors, maintaining a polished and unified look.
- Implement a clear, sectioned layout with generous use of white space. Feature cards for presenting distinct content blocks like statistics, benefits, and articles, ensuring easy navigation and information digestion.
- Incorporate subtle hover effects on interactive elements such as buttons and links, alongside gentle fade-in or slide-in transitions for content sections to enhance user engagement without distraction.
